Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

TrailReplay processes GPX files entirely in your browser. Files are not uploaded to any server unless explicitly stated. Strava connection is optional and only used to import your own activities when you authorize it.

Data We Process

  • GPX files: processed locally in your browser; not uploaded by default.
  • Strava data: when you connect, we request read access to your activities to import routes. Tokens are stored in your browser and can be revoked at any time by logging out or via Strava settings.
  • Feedback messages: if you submit feedback, we process the message content and optional email to respond.

Third Parties

  • Strava: used for OAuth and activity data import according to your consent.
  • Resend: used to send feedback emails.

Your Choices

  • You can disconnect Strava at any time within the app (Logout) or in your Strava account settings.
  • You can submit feedback without providing an email; if provided, it’s used only to reply.
